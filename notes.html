Here are course notes from classes I've taken in the past. If you find any typos or other errors, please email me at sadeek.1@osu.edu, and I'd be happy to fix them as soon as I can. The grey course notes are soon to be updated. I need to polish and update them as I go! Notes here that are published are done so with the permission of the course staff, but please note that all course content is owned by Ohio State and their professors, while mistakes should be attributed to me (in transcription or interpretation -- they were not checked by instructors).

Relevant information

I take notes using Visual Studio Code and Andrew Lin's LaTeX style package (download it here, see sample project here). These documents are meant to be a close transcription of lecture, plus any additional thoughts that come to me. I hope this makes the material easy to follow, and it also helps me be a more active learner.

Some of these notes are less clean than others, because I am still learning to live-TeX, having trouble following along with the pace of the class while typing.
